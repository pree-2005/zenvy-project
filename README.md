# zenvy-project
HR Analytics dashboard project analyzing payroll costs, overtime risk, attendance patterns, and salary leakage using data-driven insights to support workforce optimization and risk control.

- Analyzed payroll cost distribution across departments and designations.
- Identified salary leakage and overtime abuse as major payroll risk drivers.
- Evaluated attendance patterns to detect missing and abnormal attendance behavior.
- Highlighted high-cost and high-risk employees contributing disproportionately to payroll expenses.
- Compared overtime costs by designation to identify inefficiencies.
- Built a summary table for employee-level validation and audit review.

  Key Insights 
Salary leakage and overtime abuse are the primary payroll risk drivers.
A small group of employees and departments contribute to a majority of payroll costs.
Missing attendance records strongly correlate with higher payroll risk exposure.

Recommendations
Implement stricter attendance validation and approval workflows.
Introduce overtime caps and exception-based approvals for high-risk roles.
Conduct regular payroll audits to identify ghost employees and duplicate salary payments.
Monitor high-risk employees using automated risk flags in dashboards.

Conclusion 
This HR analytics dashboard provides clear visibility into payroll costs, risk areas, and employee behavior. By leveraging data-driven controls and continuous monitoring, organizations can significantly reduce salary leakage, improve payroll accuracy, and optimize workforce costs.
